Guides For NFT Marketplace

Welcome to our marketplace! Below you will find some quick insights into what an NFT is, how you can use our platform to promote your wares, and some tips for getting your creations seen.

What are NFTs

At their highest level, an NFT (or non-fungible token) is a digital record/representation on a blockchain. The NFT corresponds to a physical entity (for example, a picture, a song, or a signed baseball card). This "digital receipt," if you will, can be sold to demonstrate ownership of an item. Alongside the "receipt," the buyer may receive an actual item (a pdf of some artwork, a link to a download, etc.), but that doesn't always have to be the case.

Pixels Here, Get Your Pixels Here!

To best help people (be they artists, sellers, businesses, or any combination of the sort) generate awareness for their wares, we will be implementing our "One Million Pixels" page. On this page, users will be able to buy as many pixels as they like and will be able to use those pixels (which will be viewable to anyone browsing the website) for advertising their offerings.

What’s In A Word

You have undoubtedly worked hard to create a visually appealing, compelling NFT. With that said – the visual is only half the battle. When listing your NFT for sale, it is helpful to write a detailed description to let prospective buyers know what the piece is and why they should be excited to buy it!
